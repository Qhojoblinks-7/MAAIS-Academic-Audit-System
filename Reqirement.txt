Q9: After you, the HOD, have checked and approved a mark, should the system lock that mark to prevent anyone from making further changes?

Yes, absolutely. The system must lock the marks after HOD approval to prevent unauthorised changes and protect data integrity. 14

Yes, it is critical. The Audit Log must record the user, date, and nature of every modification to enhance accountability. 16

Centralise Personnel Data 19


Q12: For students, what important non-grade information do you wish you could easily track over their time at school?
We need a log for Qualitative Assessment (respectful behaviour towards authorities/colleagues) and Medical Updates (dates admitted to NHU). 20


Centralise Qualitative Data 21


Q13: When preparing verified data for the official WAEC STP, what is the hardest part of that process right now?
The system must be able to: 1) Handle internal score conversion (e.g., converting class exercises/notes to the required 20%). 2) Match WAEC's specific assessment groups (Class Exercise, Exam Group, Vertical Portfolio). 3) Generate an exportable script that can be easily downloaded and uploaded directly to the WAEC STP. 22
